Examples:

  • StoryMaps

    • A program similar to Powerpoint that let's you insert images, text, and videos to tell a story.

  • Omeka
  • Twine

    • A tool most commonly used in creative writing that can also be used to make games

  • ArcGIS Story

    • A program in which you can create maps through ArcGIS, as well as insert videos and images to tell a story

    It is more complex than StoryMaps

  • YouTube

    • Create your own vlog, or educational videos and upload them to your channel
